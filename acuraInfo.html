About Us

Acura is the luxury vehicle division of the Japanese automaker Honda. It was introduced to the United States and Canada in March 1986, making it one of the first luxury brands from Japan to enter the North American market. The creation of Acura was part of Honda's strategy to establish itself as a global automotive leader and compete with other luxury brands, particularly from Europe and the United States.
The development of Acura began in the early 1980s, and the brand was officially launched in 1986 with two initial models: the Acura Legend sedan and the Acura Integra sports coupe. These vehicles were well-received for their combination of performance, comfort, and advanced technology. The Legend, in particular, played a crucial role in establishing Acura's reputation for luxury and reliability.
In the following years, Acura expanded its lineup by introducing new models, including SUVs and performance-oriented vehicles. The Acura NSX, introduced in the early 1990s, was a groundbreaking sports car that showcased Honda's engineering prowess and innovation. The NSX featured advanced technologies, such as an aluminum body, a mid-engine layout, and Honda's VTEC (Variable Valve Timing and Lift Electronic Control) system.
Acura continued to evolve and innovate, introducing new technologies and design concepts to stay competitive in the luxury automotive market. Over the years, Acura has produced a range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, and crossovers, catering to different consumer preferences.
Despite facing competition from other luxury brands, Acura has maintained a reputation for reliability, performance, and value for money. The brand has also embraced technological advancements, incorporating features such as SH-AWD (Super Handling All-Wheel Drive) and AcuraWatch, a suite of safety and driver-assistance technologies.
The Acura brand has undergone various changes and updates over the years, adapting to market trends and consumer preferences. While the specific models and technologies have evolved, Acura remains a key player in the luxury automotive segment, with a focus on delivering a premium driving experience.
